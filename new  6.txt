        </tr>
          <tr>
            <td class="text"><p>For the past 35 years The Friendship Club has provided a meeting place for the residents of Belhelvie parish. Members are 50+ and meet in the lounge of Eigie House in Balmedie on alternate Tuesday afternoons from 2-4pm. A very varied programme is enjoyed which includes illustrated talks, musical entertainment, whist, dominoes, bingo, beetle drives and quizzes.</p>

                               <p> A summer outing and a Christmas Party are also enjoyed, and our regular meetings always finish off with a welcome cup of tea and a biscuit, followed by a raffle.</p>

<td><h3>Kirk Klub</h3></td>
</tr>
  <tr>
    <td class="text"><p>The Kirk Klub meets each Thursday in The Forsyth Hall from 7.15 – 9.00 PM. We aim to help young people from age 10 upward learn about what it means to be a Christian today and to develop their own relationship with God.  We do this through discussion, bible study, games and activities.  </p>

<p>We charge 50p per week and have a tuck shop, which makes a small profit.  The money we take in is used to pay for resources such as teaching materials, craft materials and equipment and prizes for the end of session Gift Service.  </p>

<p>KK also go collecting for Christian Aid every year and finish up after a chilly night with chips at the Forsyth hall.  We have also had film and pizza nights, barbecues, trips to the cinema and to the panto.</p>
<p> Email <a href = "mailto:daphne@denviewplus.com?Subject=Kirk%20Klub"> Daphne McWilliam</a> or call 01358 743469</p>
